What is a SIT?

To buy tokens of athletes and clubs on the platform, the main SIT token is used, which is traded on crypto exchanges. The token has been launched on the Binance Smart Chain network (BEP20) and has already been verified by the Binance Smart Chain Explorer validator.

In the third quarter of 2021, the token will become a multi-chain and will be available in the Freeton, Ethereum, Tron blockchains, using the development of our SIT connecting bridge team.

Date of listing on a decentralized exchange: 15.08.2021
